Abobo's Big Adventure

Abobo's Big Adventure is a free-to-play browser-based game that pays homage to classic 8-bit and 16-bit video games, particularly those from the NES era. The game features Abobo, a character from the Double Dragon series, on a quest to rescue his kidnapped son.

Players navigate through various levels, each inspired by different classic games, battling enemies and bosses along the way. The game is known for its retro graphics, nostalgic references, and humorous take on the gaming culture of the 80s and 90s.

  • Genre: Action, Adventure
  • Platform: Browser-based
  • Release Date: 2012
  • Developer: Abobo's Big Adventure Team
